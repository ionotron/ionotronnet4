Frequently Asked Questions

This page is updated regularly. Perhaps tomorrow you will find new interesting discussions here.

Cooling capacity

What is the cooling capacity of air conditioning systems based on Air Ion Technology?

Direct Free Cooling

IONOTRON is not a refrigerating machine and its efficiency is not determined by the "cooling capacity" index. On the contrary, IONOTRON provides optimal conditions for refusing to use mechanical or adiabatic refrigeration units and shifting to the wide use of the natural "cold" atmosphere.

Hardware

What IT-equipment of data centers (servers, storage systems, switches, etc.) is compatible with air-conditioning systems based on Air Ion Technology?

ASHRAE-2011 classes A2, A3, A4 

All modern equipment for data centers is manufatured according to the ASHRAE-2011 Directive. The higher class of climatic performance of IT equipment (from A2 to A4), the wider zone of effective application of the Year-round Direct Free Cooling mode. The maximum effect is achieved by using special "hot" servers, the performance characteristics of which exceed the requirements of ASHRAE.

Safety

Does the ionization of air have a negative impact on the personnel and equipment of data centers?

Complete safety for personnel and equipment

The required ionization level provided by IONOTRON does not exceed the sanitary norms according to SanPiN 2.2.4.1294-03. With regard to electrical safety and electromagnetic compatibility Air Ion equipment was tested by an independent testing laboratory and received a positive conclusion.

Compatibility with data center management systems

Is it possible to connect IONOTRONs to data center management systems?

All-inclusive

The IONOTRON controller has all the necessary interfaces (snmp, a set of control and control signals DI and DO at 24 VDC) to integrate with data center management systems.

Resource

What is the resource of Air Ion equipment? Do special training and equipment for its maintenance be required?

Up to 14 years

In the basic version, the resource of IONOTRON is 7 years of continuous operation. In addition, a special version with a resource of 14 years is available for ordering. Special training and equipment for the maintenance of IONOTRON is not required.
