Air Ion Technology implementation economic effect assessment

Reliability

There is practically impossible to develop a universal interindustry calculation algorithm of the new technology implementation economic effect, as it requires integration of a huge array of dissimilar facts. However, this problem can be formalized and correctly solved for any (type of) user. Economic effect assessment of reducing accidents damage at fuel and energy complex production facilities is given below.

According to Rostekhnadzor and Ministry of the Russian Federation for Civil Defense, Emergencies and Elimination of Consequences of Natural Disasters fuel and energy complex production facilities suffer from approximately 175 major accidents annually with an average incident damage of $37m. DCS failure is one of accident causes. 

Normally systems of this type comprise three elements: computer/controller + uninterruptible power supply + communication device. Theoretical calculation and statistics on GAZPROM production facilities give about the same probability value for such hardware complex full-year uptime: 0.37.

In the framework of the considered problem upper limit of operating-temperature range is of interest. During development of IONOTRON-DC engineering solution the logical choice was made: the “weakest” device operating temperature, i.e. +35°С to be base switching point
The presence of above mentioned temperature threshold caused the need for two IONOTRON-DC design solutions:

FREECOOL+35

Data center conditioning system temperate climate version
T ≤ Tmax = 35°С.
Air flow rate 1 м³/s per 12 kW of waste heat (standard, without additional support)

FREECOOL++

Data center conditioning system hot climate version
T > Tmax = 35°С.
Air flow rate 1 м³/s per 6 kW of waste heat (enhanced, with additional support)

The following ventilators were used in design model: for FREECOOL+35 modification – 2 axial ventilators ebm-papst S8D800-CD01-01 (Power input 0.990 kW, Air flow 18000 м³/h, Pressure increase 105 Pa); for FREECOOL++ modification – 2 axial ventilators ebm-papst W3GZ50-FC04-01 (Power input 4.700 kW, Air flow 36000 м³/h, Pressure increase 190 Pa).

Competitive advantages and prospects of implementation

DC Electronic Equipment Reliability

The transition from indirect methods (air moisturizing etc.) to physical processes of direct control of electrostatic charges formation and accumulation allows to achieve 100% triboelectric discharge radio-electronic equipment protection.

Recommended for implementation at facilities:

State-owned and corporate data centers with high availability requirements;
Hardware DCS of nuclear power plants, fuel and energy complex etc.;
Hardware military purpose automation system.

Power Efficiency

The use of controlled forced air ionization instead of air moisturizing allows to change electronic equipment air cooling modes fundamentally (to implement year-round direct free cooling and other power efficient processes).

Recommended for implementation at facilities:

Data centers, hardware DCS and communications centers with independent or limited power sources;
Power efficient Direct Free Cooling data centers;
Prospective energy-loaded super servers, basic level supercomputers.

Mobility, Independence

Air moisturizing abandonment removes requirements for equipment room air conditioners connecting to water supply and sewerage systems. Air Ion Cooling Technology implementation provides highest level of mobility and independence for electronic equipment complexes such as data or remote communication center.

Recommended for implementation at facilities:

Mobile special-purpose self-contained data centers, hardware automatic control systems and communications centers;
Stand-by and disaster-proof data centers, hardware automatic control systems and communications centers.
